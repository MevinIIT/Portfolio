From Data to Decisions: My AI Journey

I am a second-year undergraduate pursuing BSc (Hons) in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at the Informatics Institute of Technology, affiliated with Robert Gordon University. My passion lies in leveraging data to build intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. With a strong foundation in programming, machine learning, and database management, I aim to create impactful AI solutions.

My academic journey has been marked by hands-on projects like the Coronary Artery Disease Prediction System, where I led the development of a full-stack web application with Flask and integrated machine learning models for 10‑year risk prediction. I also built a Database System for a Hospital using MySQL, and developed tools like an Income Recording System in Python and JavaFX. These experiences have honed my skills in Python, Java, HTML/CSS/JS, and cloud platforms like AWS.

Beyond coding, I value collaboration and continuous learning. My background in music has taught me discipline, creativity, and effective communication—qualities that I bring into every team project. I am excited to contribute to innovative data science and AI initiatives that make a difference.
